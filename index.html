One Frog A Hour is a service that every hour, sends you a picture of a random frog.

To sign up, you must read the rules.

RULES

1. You must own the email.

Which means, it cannot be your MOTHERS email.

2. No school emails.

This is so people don't look at frogs in school, after all school is meant for learning!

3. No company owned emails.

This is so you don't get in trouble with any company, infact WE dont want to.

4. No .gov emails.

This is obvious. We do not want to get involved with the government, this is just a simple project.

5. External domains

Any domain name that is not related to a email service will be considered breakiing either rule 2 or 3, and will be suspected as spam, and will have you blacklisted.

APPEALING GUIDE

Appealing is simple, if you get blacklisted, you can either:

1. Appeal to get unblacklisted

2. Appeal for a REASON of why you got blacklisted, as this info can only be requested and not sent with your blacklist notice due to security.

3. Both 1 and 2

Keep appeals short and simple, here is a simple guide

Dear the OneFrogAHour team,

I request a (reason/removal of my blacklist) because I feel like I was falsely moderated.

HOW YOU COULD GET BLACKLISTED

Blacklists can happen for many reasons, most being hate speech, or replying to the bot (WITH hate speech), or flooding the support forum.

Certain actions can result in certain bans, some being appealable, some not.

Reputation matters too.
